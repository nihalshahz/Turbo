Features of Turbo v0.5


Natural Language Processing

Natural Language Processing (NLP) enables Turbo to understand and respond to text or voice inputs, conduct conversations as chatbots, and translate languages.



Object Recognition

Object recognition technology allows Turbo to identify and classify objects within images or videos, enabling tasks such as visual search, automated tagging, and enhanced image analysis.



Decision-making

Decision-making capabilities in Turbo involve analyzing data and providing actionable recommendations to support informed choices and strategic planning.
